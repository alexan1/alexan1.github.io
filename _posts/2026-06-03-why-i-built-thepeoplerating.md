Every day, people discuss politicians, business leaders, celebrities, journalists, influencers, and other public figures. Opinions are scattered across social media, news websites, forums, and comment sections, making it difficult to understand overall public sentiment.

I created **ThePeopleRating** to provide a simple and transparent way for people to express their opinions about public figures through ratings.

You can visit the site here:

**https://thepeoplerating.com**

## Why I Built It

Traditional social media platforms focus on posts, comments, and discussions. While these conversations can be valuable, they do not provide a simple way to measure how people feel about a specific public figure over time.

ThePeopleRating aims to solve this by allowing users to rate public figures and view the collective opinions of others.

## What You Can Do

With ThePeopleRating, users can:

- Rate public figures using a simple scoring system
- View overall ratings
- Compare different individuals
- Explore public opinion in one place

## Who Can Be Rated?

The platform focuses on public figures, including:

- Politicians
- Business leaders
- Celebrities
- Journalists
- Influencers
- Athletes
- Other notable public personalities

## Transparency and Community

The goal is not to tell people what to think. The goal is to provide a platform where users can express their opinions and see how others rate the same individuals.

As the community grows, ratings may reveal interesting trends and changes in public perception over time.

## Looking Ahead

ThePeopleRating is still evolving. Future improvements may include:

- More detailed profiles
- Historical rating trends
- Better search and discovery
- Community-requested features
