Every day people discuss politicians, business leaders, celebrities, and other public figures online. Yet there is no simple place where people can express their overall opinion through ratings.

I created ThePeopleRating to provide a platform where users can rate public figures and explore public sentiment.

## The Problem

Opinions are spread across social media, news websites, and forums. It is difficult to understand how people feel about a particular public figure at a glance.

## The Solution

ThePeopleRating allows users to:

- Rate public figures
- View overall ratings
- Compare public figures
- Discover trends in public opinion

## Looking Ahead

The project is still evolving, and user feedback will help shape future features.

Thank you for visiting and supporting ThePeopleRating.
